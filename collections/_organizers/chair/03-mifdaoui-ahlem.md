bio: >-
    Ahlem Mifdaoui is Full Professor within the Department of Complex System Engineering, ISAE-SUPAERO, University of Toulouse. She received the M.E. degree in computer science and air traffic management from the Ecole Nationale de Aviation Civile (ENAC), Toulouse, in 2004, and the Ph.D. degree in computer science and telecommunication from the Institut National Polytechnique of Toulouse (INPT) in 2007. Her main research interests are real-time networks and cyber-physical systems. Particular attention is given to the performance analysis of safety-critical applications, such as avionics and satellites.  She has been involved in almost 50 conference program committees, such as RTSS, RTNS, and RTAS and the technical program co-chair of ETFA-T3 twice and WFCS 2022 as well as general co-chair of WFCS2024.
