    Luigi De Simone is an Assistant Professor (tenure track) at Federico II University of Naples in the Department of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering. His research interests include Dependability (in particular Fault Injection Testing) of Operating Systems and Cloud Systems. He is a member of DESSERT (DEpendable and Secure Software Engineering and Real-Time Systems) research group. 
    Furthermore, he collaborates actively as researcher with CINI laboratory (Consorzio Interuniversitario Nazionale per l'Informatica) of University of Naples Federico II.
